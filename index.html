About Us

We are Doberman breeders in Swartz Creek, Michigan. Our Dobermans are part of our family and live in our home. We have a litter or two a year, due to the time and devotion needed to properly care for the puppies.

Our mommas will NEVER be sold once they are out of the breeding stage of their lives. We love them way too much!

Each puppy is handled daily to provide early Neurological Stimulation Exercises and Early Scent Introduction (ESI). ESI is the daily introduction of new scents to improve brain function. The puppies begin desensitization exercises around three to six weeks of age. Desensitization exercises are the gentle exposure of puppies to a variety of new smells, people, sounds, and items.

Our parent dogs are health tested and CLEARED for 262 genetic health conditions, INCLUDING the following 7 breed related conditions:

DCM1, DCM2, vWD, DVDob / DINGS, Ehlers Danlos, Narcolepsy, and Unilateral Deafness & Vestibular Syndrome

The puppies are from 100% European Championship bloodlines. We use Wolfgang Dobermans for our studs.

Our puppies go to their new homes with their tails docked, dewclaws removed, first set of shots, and ears cropped (optional).

Dr. Anne Midgarden, DVM, from Ohio performs the ear cropping at around 8 weeks of age.

We become very attached to our puppies (and will likely cry when they move to their forever homes). We want only the best homes for them. If for any reason, throughout the lifetime of the puppy, you can no longer care for him/her, please contact us to assist with rehoming.
